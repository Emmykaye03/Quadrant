Value-Added Services

Teaming with Quadrant Magnetics for your magnetic needs is a sound investment, not only because of quality workmanship, experienced engineers and access to the highest-quality and most reliable magnetic components, but because Quadrant Magnetics also offers a number of support services tailored to your business needs.

Material Selection

Material selection can be the most complicated step in the entire design process. Quadrant’s engineering staff is uniquely qualified to assist and recommend the best material to be used in your application. Consulting with Quadrant early in the selection process significantly increases your success. Quadrant Magnetics is at the forefront of magnetic solutions because of our expertise in “magnetics” and related designs. We can provide you with the guidance you need.

Quality Assurance

Quality is no accident. By investing in specialized magnetic test measurement equipment and training, our quality assurance department, in collaboration with our engineering staff, ensures your magnetic material or magnetic assembly meets or exceeds your performance expectations. While not every magnetic design is subjected to its maximum physical and magnetic limits, Quadrant Magnetics always tests materials according to our stringent quality assurance procedures.
